How to Play

1
Choose a Level

Start with The Voter Saga (L1-L4) to learn as a citizen, then advance to The Officer Saga (L5-L8) to manage elections.

2
Complete the Challenge

Each level has a unique interactive challenge — find ghost voters, classify deepfakes, simulate EVM voting, place polling booths, and more. Follow the on-screen instructions.

3
Earn Stars & Score

Your score is based on accuracy (70%), speed bonus (20%), and completion (10%). Using hints costs 10 points each. Earn up to ★★★ per level!

4
Learn the "Why"

After each level, the i ECI Insight reveals why that democratic process exists, grounded in India's Constitution (Articles 324-329).

5
Claim the Leaderboard

Complete all 8 levels, then submit your name, age, and email to the Global Civic Leaderboard. Top 50 players are displayed for all to see!

Scoring Formula: Score = (Accuracy × 70) + Time Bonus (up to 20) + Completion Bonus (10) − Hint Penalty (10 per hint)
